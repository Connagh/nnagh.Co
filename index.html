Real Life Subtitles

Project description:

This project explores the design potential for smart glasses as a tool for real time speech-to-text for users with hearing loss.

Over 25 unique designs were created and evaluated by 20 participants using qualitative and quantitative data collection over 4 design iterations.

Results found augmented reality to provide significant benefit to alike deafness / hard of hearing systems and provided justified guidance for future implentations.

Project awards:

• In progress application for CHI 2021 Late Breaking Works.
• Received grade of 95% as dissertation submission.
• Received grade of 100% as dissertation presentation.
